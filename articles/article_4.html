RGB Screen

Same places, different sensations..

There are immobile devices that have the ability to transport you wherever you want. RGB Screen is a work based on research and discovery of new shapes and colors, investigates the interaction between the sum of its colors and its lights, resulting in unexpected and disorienting images. The colors, the lights, the lines and the shapes mix and intertwine, creating a result that is not very clear, almost dreamlike.
